How To Play

Blackjack. Place a bet, get two cards, beat the dealer's hand without going over 21. Face cards = 10, Ace = 11 or 1. Hit draws, Stand holds, Double doubles your bet for one final card, Split separates a pair into two hands. Dealer stands on 17. Blackjack pays 3:2.

Texas Hold'em (heads-up). You and the bot each get 2 hole cards. Five community cards are dealt across four betting rounds (pre-flop, flop, turn, river). Make your best 5-card hand. Fold to quit the hand, Check/Call to stay, Bet/Raise to pressure. Blinds are $5/$10. Bust the bot or build your stack.
